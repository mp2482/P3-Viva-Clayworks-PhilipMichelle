Our Inspiration

The dream of Viva Clayworks began with a passion for sculpting and a heart for the community. We wanted to bring people together in an uplifting place so that they could rediscover a love for art. What started as a small, fledgling studio has blossomed into a an ecosystem of pros, students, and visionaries alike.

Pottery is a craft of connection and mindfulness. It reminds us to be patient and trust the process. Every class, every workshop and every piece reminds us of our love for clay as its own unique medium.

We believe that there is an artist inside everybody. Let us convince you!

30 Years of Viva

1. 1995 - Founded

Our studio began as a small branch of Clayworks Studio, run by our beloved Elli Grassmuck. She has over 47 years of experience and continues to blow our minds with her creations

2. 2017 - Became Independent

KJ MacAlister took over from Elli and transformed the studio into Viva- an independently-run space to host her community

3. 2019 - Opened Sister Location

Our family of cerama-siblings grew, and we opened a new space in B.C. for more artists to discover a love of pottery.

4. Today

30 years later, we are still here in the heart of Edmonton serving our community. Our beautiful studio has a class full of pottery wheels, as well as a ceramics gallery and firing room. Join us for a spirited introduction to our team of cerama-siblings.
