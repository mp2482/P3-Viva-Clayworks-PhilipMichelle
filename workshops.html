Group Workshops

At Viva Clayworks, we believe in learning by doing, and we are not afraid to get our hands a little muddy. Our upcoming workshops are designed to inspire beginners and veterans alike. We offer a welcoming space to explore new techniques, connect with the local community, and realize your love of clay.

Whether you're making your very first mug, refining your throwing skills, or experimenting with glaze, we aim to encourage your curiosity and offer some helpful guidance along the way. Take a look at our upcoming sessions and find the right opportunity to sculpt something beautiful.

Autumn's Finest

Celebrate the colours and coziness of the season with our Autumn's Finest workshop series. Experiment with textures during an afternoon of poetry inspired by the fall season. You'll learn how to maintain organic shapes in your clay and how to etch leaves and natural designs into your sculptures.

Gold, scarlet, and copper are the colours of the season! Craft soup bowls and decorative dishes to your heart's content.
