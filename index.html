Mission Statement

At Viva Clayworks, we believe that pottery is more than a craft—it’s a physical expression of the beauty within. It’s a piece of art that you can shape over lively conversation. Our studio is a community hub, where makers of all skill levels are welcome to explore their creativity. If you can picture yourself connecting with others over a few laughs and a pottery wheel, then Viva Clayworks is the place for you.

Here at Viva, every piece tells a story.

Slow down, share in the joy, and get a little muddy with us!
